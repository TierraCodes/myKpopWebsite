BTS is a K-Pop group that debuted in 2013. There are seven members, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. This group is the one that originally got me into K-Pop. I just love so many of their songs, Blood Sweat & Tears, Black Swan, The Truth Untold, and Jimin's Lie are all among my favorite songs. That being said, I really want to learn the choreographies for Dope and Fake Love. I love these songs so much and the high energy choreography looks really fun and cool!
